A brave servant has just entered the online world

Published: 16 June 2025

I see many people on the internet, and I do not like what I see.

The internet, in my opinion, should be a place for people to interact. But people do not interact. They influence each other with their stupidity. Some go naked to go viral. Some say that they got intimidated because of a four-year-old girl seeing a man entering a female bathroom. “The kid gets a wrong idea about us,” said a stupid man who thinks he is a girl now by using make-up and having fake big breasts. He was so intimidated. I wonder.  Who did actually feel threatened? In my opinion, the girl must have a scary look to the point she could make a forty-year-old man feel threatened. How stupid that man is. He was the one who made that little girl scared, and yet he blamed her.

I also hate YouTube. I do not hate it because it has stupid ads. Some ads that I have seen are ridiculous. They are stupid and ugly. Some are fake. Ads about the game do not show real pictures of the game's user interface. Some even promote features that do not exist in the game. But those are not the reasons I hate YouTube because my browser can still block those ads.

YouTube lacks consistency in enforcing its so-called Community Guidelines. It allows stupid videos as long as the channel is monetized. This is what I hate about YouTube. It allows scumbags like Andrew Tate to exist. He is a pimp. He is a criminal. Yet YouTube is okay with him.

I hate videos that do not show respect for women. Some women are making videos showing their bodies for the sake of views. Some even do orgasm. Those women have lost their minds. Now is the time when stupidities get normalized.

I can keep going on listing the stupidity of idiot people on YouTube. But I do not have time for that.

What do you think about the news? Fake news media exist. You can not believe everything you read on the news media. But you can not be deceived easily if you have knowledge. If you use your brain, you will have a defense against fake news. Do you want an example? There is a video showing Elon Musk balancing two spoons and the stupid reporter asked a very stupid question, “Does this video prove that Elon Musk was high in front of President Trump?” If you ask yourself, how come a man who was losing balance could balance two spoons with his little finger? Can you believe in such garbage? I do not think so.

Forget about that garbage. Let us discuss personal websites or indie web.

Personal websites are the best place to know a person even deeper. I believe everything about the website, starting from the look to the content, reflects the identity of the owner. If a person loves simplicity, this person keeps the layout of the website simple. An expressive person may use lots of emojis in their writing—but that is not always the case, right? I am aware that the internet can be a place for fake people too. People can use ChatGPT to generate content. They become too lazy to even type. In the old days, people act differently on the internet. They may have a cool username. But when I meet them, they are not that spectacular. In the end, the true nature of a person can not be seen easily just based on the person’s personal website.

Let me tell you this. I am nobody. I am not a king for sure. I am not rich. I am Gallant Servant. I chose that name because I want to serve people with a lot of great knowledge. I love writing and helping people. Having this website helps me do what I love. In case you do not know “gallant,” it means brave or bold. I want to share knowledge with strong language. I also want to face the world of this wicked internet with boldness. But I do not want to be cocky. As a result, I decided to become a brave servant. I want to be humble because I am a Christian.

I hope you get a general idea about who I am. I do not introduce myself like most people do. I imagine myself giving a speech in front of people. If you are interested in me, feel free to contact me. Don't be shy. I will not bite you—heck I can not even bite you anyway.
